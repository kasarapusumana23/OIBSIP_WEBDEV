Biography

Pichai Sundararajan, popularly known as Sundar Pichai, is an Indian-American business executive and the Chief Executive Officer of Google and Alphabet Inc.

Born in Chennai, India, Sundar Pichai completed his engineering degree from IIT Kharagpur before pursuing higher studies at Stanford University and the University of Pennsylvania.

He joined Google in 2004 and played a crucial role in the development of products such as Google Chrome, Google Drive, Gmail, and Android. Under his leadership, Google has continued to innovate and expand globally.

Achievements

  • Became CEO of Google in 2015.
  • Became CEO of Alphabet Inc. in 2019.
  • Led the development of Google Chrome.
  • Expanded Android into the world's leading mobile OS.
  • Named among the world's most influential leaders.
  • Helped shape Google's AI and cloud innovations.

Famous Quote
